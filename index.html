How To Order On Avolak

Sign Up

First, sign up on Avolak with your email and phone number. If you already have an account, then sign in.

Copy

Search for the product you want to purchase on any US E-commerce website. Copy the product link.

Paste

Paste your copied link into the box on Avolak's home page and click 'Request Item'. Once you are done, wait for your request to be approved.

Receive

After placing a successful order, you will receive your product within 2 to 4 weeks. Meanwhile, you can track your shipment

Confirm

Once your request has been approved, you can make the payment in your local currency (BDT) through our website.
